About SwiftFlow

SwiftFlow was designed to feel less like a crowded trading page and more like a refined command center for SPX options. The focus is on readability, confidence, and fast access to the information that actually matters during the session.

The platform brings together performance tracking, market structure, sentiment visibility, and flow-based context inside one premium layout. Instead of scattered widgets and noisy pages, SwiftFlow organizes the session into sections that are easy to scan and visually strong.

Daily, weekly, and monthly numbers are pulled automatically from the live report feed, allowing the results section to stay current without manual edits. The market dashboard adds more context through heatmaps, chart structure, AI bias, and headline awareness.

Every element is tuned to match a sharper brand image: darker tones, smaller typography, cleaner spacing, and stronger card hierarchy — all built around the identity of SwiftFlow as a premium SPX options experience.
